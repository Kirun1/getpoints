Create A New Offer

Offer Value
(% Discount 1-100%)

To better promote your business to the charity groups, create discounts between 10% to 25% of the offer value. The greater the discount the greater the response

Offer Length
(Time Period)

You can select how long you want your FUNDI Certificates offered for and you can limit the quantity offered. Unless you are doing a limited special occasion charity promotion we recommend you select "Unlimited" for the length offered & just limit the quantity of certificates offered under the "Quantity of Offers" section. Selecting "Unlimited" will allow us to promote your business on you site until every certificate is sold

Quantity of Offers

Select the quantity of certificates you want to offer. The best way to determine the amount is by deciding how many new customers you'd like to visit your business a month that have a slight discount. Depending on the size of your business we recommend 40 to 50 certificates per month and just multiply that times the number of months you selected in "Offer Length". For example for 1 year that would be 12 months X 40 to 50 = 480 to 600 certificates.

Business Selection

OR

You can set your merchant account up to either be able to either get invites from only local charities or all charities. If you want to support only a specific charity, click on one of the charities we already work with. If you don't see them on our list, invite them to join and they can select your business once your account is live.
